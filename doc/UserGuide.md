### Register for a new user account
Step 1 : Key-in your credentials, which is your username, email and password. (Note: password must contain at least 6 characters) <br>

### Create a study group
 Step 1 : Simply click on "Create a study group" to create a study session.<br>
<img src = "https://i.ibb.co/WpYCLCV/Whats-App-Image-2021-07-25-at-17-42-21-1.jpg" width = "300" height = "600"/>

Step 2 : You will be reminded to prepare your zoom details before proceeding to key-in your session slots. Click proceed if you have them ready. <br>
<img src = "https://i.ibb.co/B4bpvgv/Whats-App-Image-2021-07-25-at-17-53-54-2.jpg" width = "300" height = "600"/>

Step 3 : Input your desired subjects and relevant details. After inputting, click create a session. <br>
<img src = "https://i.ibb.co/PtSnTLJ/Whats-App-Image-2021-07-25-at-05-48-06-3.jpg" width = "300" height = "600"/>

 You will then receive a confirmation on the details of your study sessions. <br>

After that, you can view it under your group list. <br>
